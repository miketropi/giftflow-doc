# About GiftFlow

**GiftFlow** is a WordPress plugin that gives organizations everything they need to run fundraising campaigns, collect donations, and manage donor relationships — without leaving the WordPress admin.

We built GiftFlow because fundraising software shouldn't be complicated, expensive, or locked behind a SaaS platform. If your site runs on WordPress, your donations should too.

---

## Who We Are

GiftFlow is developed and maintained by [**BePlus**](https://beplusthemes.com/), a team of WordPress developers focused on building practical, well-engineered tools for the WordPress ecosystem.

We've spent years working with nonprofits, community groups, and small organizations who needed a reliable way to accept donations — but found existing solutions either too bloated, too restrictive, or too costly. GiftFlow is our answer to that problem.

---

## What We Believe

**Fundraising tools should be accessible.** Not every organization has a budget for enterprise software. GiftFlow is open source (GPL v2) and free to use.

**WordPress is enough.** You shouldn't need a separate platform, a separate login, or a separate monthly bill to collect donations. GiftFlow runs where your site already lives.

**Extensibility matters.** Every organization is different. GiftFlow is built with WordPress hooks, filters, and a clean architecture so developers can adapt it to any workflow.

**Transparency builds trust.** Donors and organizations both deserve clarity. GiftFlow tracks every donation, logs every event, and gives admins full visibility into campaign performance.

---

## What GiftFlow Does

- **Campaign management** — Create unlimited campaigns with goals, timelines, galleries, and location support
- **Payment processing** — Accept payments via Stripe, PayPal, or direct bank transfer out of the box
- **Donor management** — Full donor profiles, donation history, tax information, and account dashboards
- **Gutenberg blocks** — 6 purpose-built blocks for building campaign pages without code
- **Developer-friendly** — Hooks, filters, and a modular architecture for custom integrations

---

## Tech at a Glance

GiftFlow is built on modern WordPress standards with a React 19 frontend, Zustand state management, and Laravel Mix for asset bundling. It supports WordPress 6.0+, PHP 7.4+, and requires an SSL certificate for payment processing.
